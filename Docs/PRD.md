# **Product Requirements Document (PRD)**

## **Project Name:** ECSVault Website MVP

**Brand:** Elevate Cyber Solutions
**Scope:** Simple landing page + contact form
**Primary Color Palette:**

* **Accent:** Purple (#C471FF range, matching logo)
* **Background:** Black / very dark gray
* **Text:** White / light gray

---

## **1. Purpose**

The purpose of this MVP is to establish a clean, high-impact online presence for **ECSVault by Elevate Cyber Solutions**.
It will serve as the company’s digital introduction — a single-page landing site that quickly communicates trust, technical mastery, and innovation in **Cloud, Cybersecurity, Automation, and AI**.

The design must be elegant, professional, and visually aligned with the ECSVault logo, using the same purple hue as the accent color.

---

## **2. Goals**

* Communicate **who ECSVault is** and **what they offer** within seconds.
* Present the **brand identity** consistently with the logo and company message.
* Encourage visitors to **get in touch** through a simple, professional contact form.
* Optimize for **clarity, performance, and conversion**.

---

## **3. Target Audience**

* Business owners, CIOs, CTOs, and IT decision-makers looking for cloud or cybersecurity consulting.
* Partners and enterprises evaluating managed service providers or automation specialists.

---

## **4. Page Structure**

* Large version of the ECSVault logo (center or left-aligned).

* Subheadline: *Modern solutions that combine security, performance, and intelligence.*
* Primary Call-to-Action: **“Contact Us”** button (accent purple).

---

### **About Section**

  * Mention leadership by *Bismark Castilla*.

## **5. Tone & Aesthetics**

**Overall Mood:**

* Confident, secure, forward-thinking.

**Design Style:**

* Dark mode design — black background, white typography, purple accent for buttons, links, and highlights.
* Clean sans-serif fonts (Inter, Poppins, or similar).
* Minimal animation (fade-in or gentle slide).
* Modern shield/keyhole motif consistent with the logo identity.

---

## **6. Success Metrics**

* The site should clearly communicate ECSVault’s value within 5 seconds.
* Minimum of 10 contact submissions per month post-launch.
* 100% responsive design on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
* Fast load time (<2s).

---

## **7. Future Expansion**

After this MVP launch, future versions can include:

* Case studies and project portfolio.
* Blog or knowledge base for cybersecurity insights.
* Client testimonials and partnerships.
* AI chatbot for pre-qualification of leads.

---

**Summary:**
The MVP focuses solely on visual identity and conversion — a **single-page design** that uses **ECSVault’s purple/black/white color scheme**, concise storytelling, and a contact form as the call to action. It’s the first, elegant digital touchpoint for Elevate Cyber Solutions.
